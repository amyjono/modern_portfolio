About Me

I taught myself to code, which has led me to get a job as a full-time Web Developer for an agency in Norwich. I previously worked as a Retoucher for a number of years, and my professional Photoshop skills really come in handy for mocking up projects or tweaks to existing imagery.

Junior Web Developer

Norwich, Norfolk

I am regularly working with a variety of technologies such as ASP.net, classic ASP, HTML, Javascript, CSS, PHP (WordPress), and Kentico CMS.

My technical day to day duties include: Implementing new features on sites, hardcoding WordPress template files, improving SEO, getting SSL certificates verified and installed, uploading and retrieving site files using FTP, implementing in-house responsive grids, adapting sites so that they are fully responsive, testing functionality and design on different devices and browsers.

I also have experience with wireframing and design, client meetings for website proposals, and technical support over the phone.

Interests

In my spare time I enjoy developing my skills further and thinking of new ideas for projects that will challenge me. I try to make some time for learning, I would like to create more personal projects using React JS, C#, or PHP. I also enjoy cycling and skiing.
